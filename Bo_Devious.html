Bo Devious

Bo Devious (@deviousnotez) is a genre-defying vocalist, producer, and contributing artist to the Better Together Music Collective, lending his voice and production support to Purpose and The Weight of Purpose. His dynamic range spans rap, melodic hip hop, hard rock, operatic passages, and screamo, bringing fearless expression and emotional depth to every collaboration. Also a collaborating artist with SynchPathway Records and the Asc3nsion Band, Bo creates music that breaks boundaries and turns every performance into a powerful human connection.
