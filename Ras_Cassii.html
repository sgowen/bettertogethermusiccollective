Ras Cassii

Ras Cassii, also known as Rastaman19, is the CEO and Founder of The Movement #19 Entertainment, a dynamic entertainment company established in 1992 in Dayton, Ohio. In association with R.A.M. Productions, Rally Up Enterprises, Jay Milli-Air Studios, SonHeat Productions, Flow Ave Productions, and Hasan Tabally International, The Movement #19 Entertainment is dedicated to delivering exceptional music, television, film, and video productions.

Through decades of perseverance, growth, and dedication, The Movement #19 Entertainment has overcome challenges and built a respected presence in the industry. Throughout its journey, the company has collaborated on projects involving legendary artists such as Roger Troutman, the Ohio Players, Slave, Lakeside, Lil Jon, Mystikal, Timbaland, Magoo, and Montell Jordan, among many others. With a passion for creativity and a commitment to excellence, Ras Cassii and The Movement #19 Entertainment continue to expand their vision, bringing audiences powerful entertainment and exciting new projects for generations to come.
