Jake Lee

Born and raised in Taylor, Texas, Jake Lee is a singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and performer whose sound blends soulful vocals with the edge and energy of rock music.

Influenced by a diverse musical upbringing that included Motown, Tejano, rap, rock, country, reggae, metal, funk, and gospel, Jake developed a unique style that reflects both his faith and life experiences.
