Nelly Hendrix

Nelly Hendrix is an independent recording artist, producer, and sound engineer originally from Bayamón, Puerto Rico, and currently based in Florida. With a passion for creating music that inspires and connects, Nelly blends elements of hip-hop, reggaeton, and contemporary urban sounds to craft records that are both relatable and authentic. His hands-on approach to music production allows him to bring his creative vision to life from concept to final mix. Having overcome significant life challenges, Nelly channels those experiences into music that reflects resilience, growth, and determination. As an artist, producer, and engineer, he is dedicated to continuously developing his craft while building meaningful connections through his music. As a member of the Together Music Collective, Nelly brings creativity, versatility, and a unique perspective shaped by both his Puerto Rican roots and his commitment to artistic excellence.
