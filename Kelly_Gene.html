Kelly Gene

Kelly Gene, known as The First Lady of Poetry, is a multidisciplinary writer, spoken word artist, playwright, and performance creator whose work bridges storytelling, music, and social impact. With over two decades of experience in poetry and live performance, she develops narrative-driven projects that center unity, healing, and the transformative power of collaboration across diverse voices. Her creative work often explores themes of resilience, mental health awareness, and community restoration, drawing from real lived experience and a deep commitment to human connection. Through projects like Better Together, she brings together emerging and established artists to create original work that reflects shared struggle and collective triumph.
