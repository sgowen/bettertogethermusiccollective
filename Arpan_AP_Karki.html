Arpan "AP" Karki

Arpan “AP” Karki is the lead vocalist, co-founder, songwriter, and creative force behind NepCali. Born in Nepal and later establishing roots in Southern California, AP has become known for his commanding stage presence, distinctive vocal style, and ability to merge diverse musical influences into a compelling artistic vision.

Drawing inspiration from both Nepalese music and Western rock traditions, AP developed a sound that reflects his multicultural journey and passion for storytelling. His leadership has helped transform NepCali from a local garage project into a respected independent act with a growing international following.

As both a performer and entrepreneur, AP has been instrumental in shaping the band's creative direction, studio development, and long-term vision. His commitment to authenticity, cultural representation, and musical excellence continues to inspire audiences while building bridges between communities through the universal language of music.

Today, AP remains dedicated to creating music that resonates across generations and cultures, proving that powerful storytelling and genuine artistry can connect people from every walk of life.
