DAPHYA

DAPHYA is a Los Angeles-based rapper, singer, songwriter, podcaster, and entrepreneur whose work blends creativity with a passion for personal development.

Drawing from her background in behavioral psychology, she creates music and content that promote self-awareness, authenticity, and growth. As the founder of DAPHYA Shop and creator of Non-Toxic Positivity, DAPHYA empowers others to move beyond limiting beliefs, pursue purpose over approval, and embrace meaningful change through her music, podcast, and inspiring online presence.
