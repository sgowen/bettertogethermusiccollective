EyeAmCelebrity

Born Joshua Pellum in Houston, Texas, EyeAmCelebrity is an emerging recording artist, songwriter, rapper, and performer whose signature sound, known as “Lift Off Musik,” blends socially conscious storytelling with authentic street perspective.

Influenced by legendary artists such as UGK, MJG, Outkast, and Nas, Celebrity creates music designed to elevate listeners beyond their everyday experiences. His growing career has included opening performances for major artists including Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, Tory Lanez, Russ, Gucci Mane, XXXTentacion, Zoey Dollaz, and Young Greatness, while partnering with Dallas radio powerhouses 97.9 The Beat and K104. Known for his innovative pop-up performances throughout downtown Dallas, Celebrity embraces the philosophy that the world itself is a stage. A former student of Prairie View A&M University, where his musical journey began through dorm-room freestyles, he remains committed to using his platform to inspire change and give back to underserved communities through charitable outreach. With projects such as Lift Off No Landing, Musik From Marz, and Chapters After Lift Off, Celebrity continues to establish himself as one of the South's most promising independent voices.
