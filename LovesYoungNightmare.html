Loves Young Nightmare

Loves Young Nightmare is an experimental alternative artist whose music explores the space between longing, melancholy, and hope.

Drawing inspiration from artists across genres-from Lana Del Rey and Hayley Williams to Lil Peep and The Doors—she blends atmospheric vocals, emotional storytelling, and unconventional influences into a sound that feels both nostalgic and unfamiliar. The name Loves Young Nightmare is inspired by a line from A Clockwork Orange, reflecting the tension between romantic ideals and the realities that often replace them.
