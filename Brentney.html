Brentney

Brentney is a Santa Fe-based genre-bending artist blending soul, indie, and dream pop through sultry vocals and introspective songwriting.

A vocalist, songwriter, storyteller, and baritone ukulele player, she first led the indie project Patchwork Moonlight in Bloomington, Indiana before releasing her solo debut album Bluesky No. 9 in 2021. She is currently developing new music while continuing to shape her evolving sound as an independent artist.
