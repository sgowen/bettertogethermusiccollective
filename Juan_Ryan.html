Juan Ryan

Juan Ryan is an artist and songwriter from Sacramento, California, whose passion for music began at just nine years old.

Inspired by the honesty and emotional depth of Tupac Shakur and driven by the lyrical mastery of Eminem, Juan developed a deep appreciation for wordplay, punchlines, double entendres, and meaningful storytelling. Rooted in authenticity and positivity, he has spent years refining his craft and finding his voice. After overcoming self-doubt and learning to embrace his unique sound, Juan has stepped fully into his artistry, creating music that reflects who he truly is while inspiring others to do the same.
